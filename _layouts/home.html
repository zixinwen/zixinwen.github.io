About Me

I am an Undergraduate of Statistics department in UIBE Beijing.

My research interests lie broadly in machine learning, optimization and statistics theory. More specifically.

  • Statistics theory: Empirical Process Theory, High-dimensional Statistics, Nonparametrics;

  • Machine Learning: theory of Deep Learning and Interpretability;

  • Optimization: Nonconvex optimization methods and theory.
